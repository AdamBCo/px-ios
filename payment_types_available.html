Payments Types and Payment Methods available

Payment types and methods will differ according the public key of your country. You can use this payment info to exclude payment types and methods in a preference and in the checkout flow.

Payment Types

The payment types available are:

  • Credit Card
  • Debit Card
  • Prepaid Card
  • Account Money
  • Ticket
  • Bank Transfer
  • Atm
  • Digital Currency or Bitcoin

Payment Methods Available

Check the payment types available for your country with the following API.

For example if you want to check out the payment methods available for Brasil you need to complete the api call with siteId as MLB.

You can exclude payment methods in the checkout flow, specifying those payment method ids in the excludedPaymentMethods attribute when you can create a CheckoutPreference.
